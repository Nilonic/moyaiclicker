Moyai Clicker Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy aims to provide comprehensive information regarding the use of cookies by Moyai Clicker ("we", "us", or "our") on our website and within our game.

What are cookies?

Cookies represent small pieces of data that are stored on your device, whether it be a computer or mobile device, when you interact with a website or a game. These cookies serve various purposes, including enhancing the efficiency of websites and games and furnishing information to the owners of such websites or games.

Types of cookies we use

In Moyai Clicker, we exclusively utilize essential cookies. These cookies are indispensable for the proper operation of the game and cannot be opted out of. The essential cookies we employ include:

  • cur_theme: This cookie retains the user's current theme selection.
  • cookiesClickOK: It signifies whether the user has consented to the usage of cookies by clicking the "OK" button.
  • curMoyaiClickd: Tracks the number of clicks on the Moyai statue within the game.
  • curVolume: This cookie stores the volume you set

If you have inquiries regarding our Cookie Policy or our practices, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. You can contact us by visiting our GitHub page at https://github.com/nilonic/moyaiclicker.
